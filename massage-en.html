At Spa Deluxe, we offer a comprehensive range of massages and wellness treatments that can be booked as an addition to your private spa reservation. Whether you come as a couple or plan a wellness gathering for a small group, we will prepare a quality program tailored to your comfort.

Every massage begins with a short professional consultation with a certified therapist, who will recommend the most suitable technique based on your needs — from deep relaxation to body regeneration. Our offer also includes sauna and aroma ceremonies with premium dōTERRA aromatherapy, traditional sauna rituals with whisking, and cosmetic body treatments with Italian professional cosmetics Comfort Zone.

All treatments take place in a private spa environment, with an emphasis on peace, quality, and maximum comfort.

The 'Wellness Base' is a private program during which you have the entire spa space reserved exclusively for yourself. An ideal choice for couples, families, or small groups seeking peace, privacy, and quality relaxation.
